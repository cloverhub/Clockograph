The Clockograph is a functioning photographic timepiece made entirely of photos I have taken in over 30 countries around the world. Each photo represents one of the 1440 minutes of the day, with the current time evident somewhere in the photo. You might find the time on a clocktower, on a wristwatch in a crowd, or even on a sundial. Nothing is set up or posed. Every person is a stranger.
